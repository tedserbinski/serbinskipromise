The Serbinski Promise

How can I help you?

Created by Ted Serbinski

The Serbinski Promise™ originally started at Detroit Venture Partners, when Partner, Ted Serbinski, originally promised his team he'd make their lives better by switching them from Microsoft Exchange to Google Apps. At first, there was resistance, pushback, and cursing. However, a few weeks later, everyone was amazed at how much more productive they were and everything just worked. Their lives were better!

And since that fateful day in 2012, Ted Serbinski has been helping everyone he meets, living up to the fabled Serbinski Promise™.
